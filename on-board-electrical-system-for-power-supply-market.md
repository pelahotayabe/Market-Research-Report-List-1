<p><p>The On-Board Electrical System for Power Supply market comprises two main types: On-Board AC Electrical Systems and On-Board DC Electrical Systems. On-Board AC Electrical Systems utilize alternating current for powering appliances and equipment, offering efficiency and compatibility with larger loads, typically found in commercial aircraft and ships. Conversely, On-Board DC Electrical Systems utilize direct current, favored for its simplicity and reliability, making it ideal for smaller vehicles and applications such as automotive and aerospace electronics, where weight and energy efficiency are critical.</p></p>
