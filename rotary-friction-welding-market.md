<p><strong>The Rotary Friction Welding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inertia Rotary Friction Welding</li><li>Continuous Drive Rotary Friction Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rotary Friction Welding market comprises two main types: Inertia Rotary Friction Welding and Continuous Drive Rotary Friction Welding. Inertia Rotary Friction Welding utilizes a flywheel to store energy, releasing it during the welding process to create a strong bond through friction. Conversely, Continuous Drive Rotary Friction Welding employs a continuously driven motor, allowing for a consistent weld speed and greater control over the welding parameters. Both methods are essential for creating durable joints in various industrial applications.</p></p>
