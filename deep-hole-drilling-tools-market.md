<p><strong>The Deep Hole Drilling Tools Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gun Drilling</li><li>BTA / STS</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deep hole drilling tools are categorized based on their drilling techniques and applications. Gun drilling involves a long, thin tool that creates precise, deep holes with minimal deviation, ideal for manufacturing components requiring high accuracy. BTA (Boring and Trepanning Association) and STS (Single Tube System) methods employ a single-tube design to efficiently remove chips from deep holes, enhancing drilling speed and precision. Other types include alternate techniques like pull boring, often utilized in specialized applications requiring unique hole characteristics.</p></p>
