<p><ul><li>Fixed Wing Professional Drone</li><li>Rotary Wing Professional Drone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The professional drone market primarily consists of two types: fixed-wing and rotary-wing drones. Fixed-wing drones resemble airplanes and are ideal for covering large areas efficiently, often used in mapping, agriculture, and surveillance. They require runways for takeoff and landing. In contrast, rotary-wing drones, commonly known as quadcopters, feature vertical lift and can hover in place. They are widely utilized for aerial photography, inspections, and logistics. Each type serves distinct applications based on operational needs and flight capabilities.</p></p>
