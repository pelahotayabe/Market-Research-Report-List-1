<p><p>High-Speed Direct Attach Copper (DAC) cables are essential for high-frequency data transmission in data centers and networking applications. They come in two primary types: passive and active DAC cables. Passive DAC cables rely on the physical design to transmit signals without additional boosts, making them cost-effective for shorter distances. In contrast, active DAC cables incorporate signal amplification, allowing for longer distances and higher performance at the expense of slight increases in cost and power consumption, catering to varying connectivity needs.</p></p>
