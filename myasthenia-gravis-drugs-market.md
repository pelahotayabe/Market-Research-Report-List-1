<p><ul><li>Anticholinesterases</li><li>Immunosuppressants</li><li>Intravenous Immune Globulins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Myasthenia Gravis drugs market consists of several key categories. Anticholinesterases, such as pyridostigmine, enhance neuromuscular transmission by inhibiting the breakdown of acetylcholine, effectively improving muscle strength. Immunosuppressants, like corticosteroids and other agents, reduce autoimmune responses that attack the body's receptors. Intravenous immune globulins (IVIG) provide immediate relief by modulating the immune system, offering short-term benefits during exacerbations. Together, these treatments aim to alleviate symptoms and improve the quality of life for individuals with Myasthenia Gravis.</p></p>
