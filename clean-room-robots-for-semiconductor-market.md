Vacuum robots are utilized in scenarios requiring extremely low particle counts, often employing vacuum technology to remove contaminants effectively. In contrast, atmospheric robots operate in standard atmospheric conditions, equipped with features to handle sensitive materials without the risk of contamination typically associated with human handling. Both types enhance efficiency and precision in semiconductor manufacturing processes while ensuring compliance with cleanliness standards.</p></p>
