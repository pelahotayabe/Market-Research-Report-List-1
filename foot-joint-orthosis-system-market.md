<p><p>The Foot Joint Orthosis System market comprises two main types: Inlay Type and Externally Assisted. </p><p>Inlay Type orthoses are designed to fit inside footwear, providing support and alignment to the foot's structure. They enhance comfort and stability during movement. </p><p>Externally Assisted orthoses, on the other hand, are worn externally and offer more substantial support. They can include braces or splints that stabilize the foot joint through external mechanics, aiding in rehabilitation or managing chronic conditions.</p></p>
