<p><p>The cellular imaging market is divided into microscopic and macroscopic techniques. Microscopic techniques, including light microscopy and electron microscopy, provide detailed views of cellular structures and functions at the micro-level, allowing for in-depth analysis. In contrast, macroscopic techniques involve imaging at larger scales, such as whole organ systems or tissues, using methods like MRI or CT scans. Both approaches play crucial roles in research and clinical diagnostics, enabling better understanding of cellular behaviors and disease mechanisms.</p></p>
