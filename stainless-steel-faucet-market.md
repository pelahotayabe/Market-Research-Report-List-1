Single joint style faucets typically feature a simple design with one handle for temperature control, making them user-friendly. Double joint style faucets offer increased maneuverability with two movable joints, ideal for deeper sinks. Triple joint style faucets are designed with three joints, providing maximum flexibility and reach, often used in commercial settings for enhanced functionality and ease of use.</p></p>
