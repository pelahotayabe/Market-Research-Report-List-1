<p><p>The metamaterials for antenna market encompasses various types. Negative-index metamaterials (NIM) facilitate wave manipulation, enabling antennas to achieve enhanced performance. Single negative (SNG) metamaterials possess either negative permittivity or permeability, optimizing signal transmission. Hyperbolic metamaterials (HMMs) support multiple propagation modes, offering improved bandwidth and efficiency for antennas. Other types may include anisotropic metamaterials and engineered composites that tailor specific electromagnetic properties, further diversifying applications in telecommunications and wireless technologies, combating challenges in antenna design and performance.</p></p>
