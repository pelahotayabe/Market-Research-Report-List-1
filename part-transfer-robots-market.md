SCARA robots excel in lateral movement, ideal for assembly and packaging. Delta robots, known for their high-speed operations, are perfect for picking and placing lightweight items. Cartesian robots utilize linear movements along axes, making them effective for precise positioning in tasks like CNC machining. Each type serves distinct roles in automation and efficiency.</p></p>
