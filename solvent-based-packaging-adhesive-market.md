<p><p>The solvent-based packaging adhesive market is divided into two main types: one-component and two-component adhesives. One-component adhesives are ready to use, requiring no mixing, and are ideal for quick applications and ease of use. In contrast, two-component adhesives involve a mixing process, combining a resin and hardener to achieve stronger bonds, making them suitable for more demanding applications. Each type offers distinct advantages, addressing diverse packaging needs across various industries, such as food, consumer goods, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
