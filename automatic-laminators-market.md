<p><ul><li>Two Side Laminators</li><li>One Side Laminators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic laminators market is divided into two main types: two-side laminators and one-side laminators. Two-side laminators apply a protective film on both sides of a substrate, enhancing durability and providing a professional finish, suitable for items like menus and ID cards. One-side laminators, on the other hand, coat only one side, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal protection, such as posters or flyers. Each type serves specific needs in various industries, including printing, packaging, and office supplies.</p></p>
