<p><p>The traffic signal controller market features several types based on operational efficiency. Centralized Adaptive Traffic Signal Controllers optimize signal timings in real-time based on traffic conditions, enhancing flow and reducing congestion. Fixed Time Traffic Signal Controllers operate on predetermined schedules, providing consistent timings regardless of traffic volume. Actuated Control Traffic Signal Controllers adjust signal phases based on vehicle presence detected via sensors, ensuring responsiveness to actual traffic conditions. Each type plays a crucial role in improving urban traffic management and safety.</p></p>
