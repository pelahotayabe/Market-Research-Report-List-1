<p><p>The manual hydraulic valve market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on flow control valves and pressure control valves. Flow control valves regulate the fluid flow rate in hydraulic systems, allowing for precise speed adjustments in machinery. In contrast, pressure control valves maintain the desired pressure levels, preventing system overload and ensuring operational safety. Both types are essential for optimizing performance, enhancing efficiency, and protecting equipment, making them integral to various industrial applications, including construction, manufacturing, and automotive sectors.</p></p>
