<p><p>Spin-wave logic devices leverage the manipulation of spin waves in magnetic materials for information processing. The market can be categorized into clockwise spin and counterclockwise spin types. Clockwise spin devices generate spin waves that propagate in a clockwise direction, facilitating logical operations based on controlled wave interactions. Counterclockwise spin devices operate similarly but with waves that propagate in the opposite direction. Both types offer potential advantages in speed, energy efficiency, and miniaturization for future computing technologies.</p></p>
