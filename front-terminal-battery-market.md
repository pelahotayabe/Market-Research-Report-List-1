<p><ul><li>AGM Battery</li><li>GEL Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The front terminal battery market primarily consists of AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) and GEL batteries, both designed for efficient energy storage. AGM batteries utilize absorbed electrolyte in fiberglass mats, providing high performance, low maintenance, and resilience to vibrations. They are suitable for high-drain applications. GEL batteries contain a gelified electrolyte, offering enhanced safety and deep-cycle capabilities. These are ideal for applications requiring longevity and stability under varying temperatures. Both types support renewable energy systems and uninterruptible power supplies.</p></p>
