<p><p>The digital anemometer market comprises two main types: fixed and portable anemometers. Fixed anemometers are permanently installed in specific locations, ideal for continuous airflow measurement in applications like HVAC systems and weather stations. They provide stable, long-term data but lack mobility. Portable anemometers, on the other hand, are compact and designed for field use, allowing users to measure wind speed and direction in various environments. These versatile devices cater to diverse industries, including meteorology, construction, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
