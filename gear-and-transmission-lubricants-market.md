<p><p>The Gear and Transmission Lubricants market is primarily divided into two types: synthetic oil and mineral oil. Synthetic oils are engineered for high-performance applications, offering superior thermal stability, lower friction, and enhanced protection under extreme conditions. They cater to modern vehicles and machinery. In contrast, mineral oils, derived from refined crude oil, are cost-effective and suitable for standard applications, providing adequate lubrication for traditional gears and transmissions while having a more limited temperature range and longevity compared to synthetics.</p></p>
