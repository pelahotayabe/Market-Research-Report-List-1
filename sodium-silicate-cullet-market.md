<p><p>The Sodium Silicate Cullet market is categorized into three main types based on molecular weight: Medium Mol, High Mol, and Extra-High Mol. Medium Mol sodium silicate has moderate silica-to-soda ratios suited for general applications like detergents and adhesives. High Mol offers a higher silica content, providing enhanced durability for industrial uses. Extra-High Mol consists of even higher silica ratios, making it ideal for specialized applications in ceramics and high-strength glass products, emphasizing its role in diverse industrial sectors.</p></p>
