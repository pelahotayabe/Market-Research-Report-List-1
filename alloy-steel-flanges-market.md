<p><strong>The Alloy Steel Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blind Flange</li><li>Weld Neck Flange</li><li>Slip-On Flange</li><li>Socket Weld Flange</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alloy steel flanges are essential components in piping systems, available in various types to suit different applications. Blind flanges seal the end of pipes, preventing flow. Weld neck flanges provide strong, permanent connections through welding. Slip-on flanges are easy to install, sliding over pipe ends. Socket weld flanges allow for a smooth transition between pipe and flange, minimizing turbulence. Other types may include threaded or lap joint flanges, each designed for specific installation and operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
