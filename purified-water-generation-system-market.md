<p><p>The Purified Water Generation System market consists of three main types: UV-based, RO-based, and gravity-based systems. UV-based systems utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses, ensuring safe drinking water. RO-based systems employ reverse osmosis technology to filter out impurities and contaminants effectively. Gravity-based systems rely on natural gravitational forces to filter water, typically using sedimentation and filtration processes. Each type caters to different needs and applications, ensuring access to purified water across various settings.</p></p>
