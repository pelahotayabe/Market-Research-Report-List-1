<p><ul><li>Negative Index Metamaterial (NIM)</li><li>Single Negative (SNG) Metamaterials</li><li>Hyperbolic Metamaterials (HMM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metamaterials for communication antennas are advanced materials engineered to manipulate electromagnetic waves. Negative Index Metamaterials (NIM) enable negative refraction, leading to enhanced antenna performance and reduced sizes. Single Negative (SNG) Metamaterials possess either negative permittivity or permeability, useful for specific applications like absorption or transmission. Hyperbolic Metamaterials (HMM) allow for a unique dispersion relation, supporting sub-wavelength imaging and improved signal propagation. Together, these types enhance the capabilities and effectiveness of modern communication systems.</p></p>
