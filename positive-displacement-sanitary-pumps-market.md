<p><strong>The Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps</li><li>Reciprocating Positive Displacement Sanitary Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Positive displacement sanitary pumps are designed to move fluids in hygienic applications, ensuring minimal contamination. Rotary positive displacement pumps use rotating elements to trap and transport fluid, making them ideal for viscous materials and providing a steady flow rate. Reciprocating positive displacement pumps utilize a back-and-forth motion to draw and discharge fluids, typically suited for high-pressure conditions. Both types are crucial in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology, where stringent cleanliness and accuracy are essential.</p></p>
