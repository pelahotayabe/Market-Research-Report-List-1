<p><ul><li>Interferometric Fiber Optic Gyroscope</li><li>Resonant Fiber OpticGyro</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser gyroscope market primarily comprises two types: Interferometric Fiber Optic Gyroscopes (IFOGs) and Resonant Fiber Optic Gyroscopes (RFOGs). IFOGs utilize optical interference to measure angular rotation with high precision, making them ideal for aerospace and defense applications. In contrast, RFOGs rely on resonant cavities to detect rotational motion, offering a compact design and reduced sensitivity to environmental factors. Both technologies are critical in navigation systems, robotics, and automotive applications, driving innovation and growth in the industry.</p></p>
