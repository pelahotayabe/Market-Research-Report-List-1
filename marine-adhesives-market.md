<p><p>The marine adhesives market comprises three primary types: epoxy, acrylic, and polyurethane. Epoxy adhesives are renowned for their exceptional strength and resistance to water, making them ideal for structural applications. Acrylic adhesives offer versatility and quick-setting properties, suitable for bonding various materials in marine environments. Polyurethane adhesives are flexible and durable, providing excellent resistance to moisture and UV exposure. Each type serves unique demands in shipbuilding, repairs, and maintenance, ensuring strong and reliable bonds in challenging marine conditions.</p></p>
