<p><ul><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction</li><li>Catalytic Oxidation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary catalytic systems market includes technologies like Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) and Catalytic Oxidation (CO). SCR focuses on reducing nitrogen oxides (NOx) emissions from industrial sources by converting them into harmless nitrogen and water using a catalyst and a reducing agent, typically ammonia. In contrast, Catalytic Oxidation promotes the conversion of harmful volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and carbon monoxide into less harmful substances through oxidation. Both methods aim to enhance air quality and comply with environmental regulations.</p></p>
