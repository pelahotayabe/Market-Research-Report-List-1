<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Airbag Inflator market consists of three primary types: </p><p>1. **Pyrotechnic Inflator**: Utilizes a chemical reaction to rapidly produce gas and inflate airbags upon impact. </p><p>2. **Stored Gas Inflator**: Relies on compressed gas stored in high-pressure canisters to inflate airbags when triggered. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Inflator**: Combines features of both pyrotechnic and stored gas systems, offering a more versatile and efficient inflation method tailored to the specific needs of electric vehicles.</p></p>
