<p><p>The electro-mechanical brake market includes various types tailored for specific applications. Single face brakes involve a braking surface on one side, suitable for compact designs. Power-off brakes engage when power is lost, ensuring safety in critical scenarios. Particle brakes utilize magnetic particles for smooth deceleration, ideal for precise control. Hysteresis power brakes offer high torque with minimal wear, enhancing longevity. Multiple disk brakes provide high torque and compactness, commonly used in high-performance systems. Each type addresses unique performance and safety needs in diverse industries.</p></p>
