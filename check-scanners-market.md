<p><p>The check scanners market is categorized into single-feed and multi-feed check scanners. Single-feed check scanners process one check at a time, making them ideal for low-volume environments, such as small businesses. Both types offer features like scanning resolution, speed, and processing capabilities, catering to different user needs and operational demands.</p></p>
