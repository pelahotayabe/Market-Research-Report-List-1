<p><p>The fermented tofu market primarily features two types: white preserved bean curd and red fermented bean curd. White preserved bean curd is known for its smooth texture and mild flavor, typically enjoyed in soups and as a condiment. In contrast, red fermented bean curd, colored by the addition of red yeast rice, has a richer, more complex taste, often used in marinades and as a spread. Both types cater to diverse culinary preferences and enhance the umami profile of various dishes.</p></p>
