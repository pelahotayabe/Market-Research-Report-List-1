<p><p>The Terahertz technology market encompasses two primary segments: Terahertz sources and Terahertz detectors. Terahertz sources generate electromagnetic radiation in the terahertz frequency range, utilized in various applications like imaging and spectroscopy. These sources include photoconductive antennas, quantum cascade lasers, and optical rectification devices. On the other hand, Terahertz detectors respond to terahertz waves, converting them into measurable signals. They include bolometers, photoconductive detectors, and diode-based systems, facilitating advanced sensing and imaging capabilities across industries such as telecommunications, security, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
