<p><p>The alumina balls market is categorized into three main types based on aluminum content: Medium Aluminum Balls, Moderate to High Aluminum Balls, and High Aluminum Balls. Medium Aluminum Balls typically contain around 50% to 80% aluminum oxide, offering balance between strength and cost. Moderate to High Aluminum Balls, with aluminum content ranging from 80% to 90%, provide enhanced durability and thermal resistance. High Aluminum Balls, exceeding 90% aluminum oxide, deliver exceptional hardness and chemical resistance, making them suitable for specialized industrial applications.</p></p>
