<p><p>Simplified Voyage Data Recorders (VDR) are essential maritime safety devices that capture critical navigation and operational data. The market features two main types: Float Free Capsule and Fixed Capsule. Float Free Capsules are designed to detach from the vessel and float to the surface in emergencies, ensuring data retrieval. In contrast, Fixed Capsules are permanently mounted on the ship, offering continuous data recording but lacking the buoyancy feature. Both types serve to enhance maritime safety and facilitate incident investigations.</p></p>
