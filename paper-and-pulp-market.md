<p><ul><li>Bleached Softwood Kraft Pulp (BSK)</li><li>Birch Hardwood Kraft Pulp (BHK)</li><li>High Yield Pulp (HYP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Paper and Pulp Market** includes various types of pulp used in paper production. **Bleached Softwood Kraft Pulp (BSK)** is derived from softwood trees like pine, offering high strength and brightness, ideal for premium paper products. **Birch Hardwood Kraft Pulp (BHK)** comes from hardwood trees like birch, providing smoothness and opacity, commonly used in printing and writing papers. **High Yield Pulp (HYP)** is produced with higher fiber yield, offering cost efficiency and bulk, often used in packaging and tissue papers. Each type serves distinct applications based on its properties.</p></p>
