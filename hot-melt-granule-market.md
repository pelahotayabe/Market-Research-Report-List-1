<p><p>The Hot Melt Granule market is primarily categorized into two types: EVA-based and PUR-based. EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) granules are known for their excellent adhesion properties, flexibility, and are commonly used in packaging, automotive, and electronics. In contrast, PUR (Polyurethane) granules offer superior temperature resistance and durability, making them ideal for construction, automotive, and furniture applications. Both types cater to specific industry needs, providing tailored solutions for bonding, insulation, and sealing processes across various sectors.</p></p>
