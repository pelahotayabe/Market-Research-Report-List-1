<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PA Ultrasonic Radar</li><li>APA Ultrasonic Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic radar market is primarily categorized into two types: PA (Pulse Amplitude) Ultrasonic Radar and APA (Adaptive Pulse Amplitude) Ultrasonic Radar. PA Ultrasonic Radar utilizes fixed pulse amplitude to detect distances and objects, making it suitable for various applications in level measurement and obstacle detection. In contrast, APA Ultrasonic Radar adapts its pulse amplitude for enhanced sensitivity and performance in dynamic environments, allowing for improved detection accuracy and reliability in changing conditions. Both types are integral to advancing ultrasonic technology.</p></p>
