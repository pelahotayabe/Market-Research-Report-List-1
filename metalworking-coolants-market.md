<p><p>The metalworking coolants market is primarily divided into two types: non-water soluble and water soluble coolants. Non-water soluble coolants, such as oils, provide excellent lubrication and thermal stability, making them suitable for heavy-duty machining operations. In contrast, water soluble coolants offer effective cooling and chip removal, consisting of emulsions or solutions that mix with water. They tend to be more economical and environmentally friendly, widely used in machining processes where cooling efficiency and cleanliness are essential.</p></p>
