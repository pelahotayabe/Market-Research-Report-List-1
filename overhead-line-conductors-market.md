<p><p>The overhead line conductors market includes various types tailored for specific applications. ACSR (Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced) combines aluminum strands with a steel core, enhancing strength and conductivity. AAAC (All Aluminum Alloy Conductor) offers improved corrosion resistance and lightweight properties. ACAR (Aluminum Conductor Alloy Reinforced) merges aluminum and alloy for better performance. AACSR (All Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced) features all-aluminum strands with a steel core. AAC (All Aluminum Conductor) is purely aluminum, providing excellent conductivity. Other types may include specialized or composite conductors for unique applications.</p></p>
