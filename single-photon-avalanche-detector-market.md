<p><ul><li>Line-mode</li><li>Geiger-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single Photon Avalanche Detector (SPAD) market can be categorized into line-mode and Geiger-mode types. Line-mode SPADs operate by detecting single photons in a continuous manner, making them suitable for applications requiring high timing resolution and sensitivity, such as LIDAR and fluorescence lifetime imaging. In contrast, Geiger-mode SPADs function by exploiting avalanche breakdown to achieve high gain and sensitivity, effectively counting photons but with limitations in response time. Both types cater to various sectors, including biomedical, quantum communication, and defense technologies.</p></p>
