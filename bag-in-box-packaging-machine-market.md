<p><p>Bag-in-box packaging machines are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. </p><p>Manual machines require operators to perform most tasks, offering low initial costs and flexibility but higher labor input. Semi-automatic machines streamline some processes, enhancing efficiency while still needing operator intervention. Fully automatic machines handle all packaging processes autonomously, maximizing speed and minimizing labor, making them ideal for high-volume production. Each type caters to different business needs based on budget, production scale, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
