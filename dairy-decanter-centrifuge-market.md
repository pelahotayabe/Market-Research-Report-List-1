<p><ul><li> Two-phase</li><li>Three-phase </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dairy decanter centrifuge market is segmented into two types: two-phase and three-phase centrifuges. Two-phase centrifuges separate liquid and solid phases, typically used for whey processing and separating cream from skim milk. In contrast, three-phase centrifuges separate two liquid phases (like cream and skim milk) along with solids, providing a more efficient separation for products like butterfat and skim milk. Each type is tailored to specific dairy processing needs, optimizing product yield and quality.</p></p>
