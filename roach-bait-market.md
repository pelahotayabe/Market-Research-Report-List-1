<p><p>The roach bait market comprises three main types: bait stations, granular baits, and gel baits. Bait stations are enclosed units that attract and contain roaches, delivering poison discreetly. Granular baits consist of dry granules that can be scattered in infested areas, providing a broad reach. Gel baits, on the other hand, are sticky formulations applied in targeted locations, appealing to roaches while minimizing exposure to non-target organisms. These methods cater to various consumer preferences for pest control efficiency and convenience.</p></p>
