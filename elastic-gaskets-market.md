<p><p>The elastic gaskets market includes various types, primarily saddle and waveform gaskets. Saddle gaskets, characterized by their U-shaped design, are designed to provide a secure seal in pipe connections and flanges while accommodating thermal expansion. Waveform gaskets feature a series of ridges or waves, enhancing flexibility and ensuring a tighter seal under varying pressure and temperature conditions. Both types are used across industries, including automotive and aerospace, to prevent leaks and maintain system integrity.</p></p>
