<p><p>Mold release agents are essential for preventing adhesion between molds and the materials being shaped. The market is divided into two primary types: water-based and solvent-based agents. Water-based agents are environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and safe for various applications, making them popular in industries focused on sustainability. Solvent-based agents, on the other hand, offer superior performance, providing a robust barrier and smoother finishes but can pose safety and environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds. Each type serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
