<p><p>Automated sortation systems are vital in logistics and manufacturing, enhancing efficiency in sorting items. Linear sortation systems operate in a straight-line configuration, ideal for high-speed sorting of small to medium-sized packages, often used in e-commerce fulfillment. In contrast, loop sortation systems feature a circular design, enabling continuous processing of items, making them suitable for larger packages or heavier loads. Both systems optimize space and increase throughput, catering to various operational needs within distribution centers and warehouses.</p></p>
