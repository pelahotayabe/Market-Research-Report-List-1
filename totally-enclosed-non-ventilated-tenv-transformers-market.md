<p><p>Totally Enclosed Non-Ventilated (TENV) transformers are a type of electrical transformer designed for operation in harsh environments, where protection from dust, moisture, and other contaminants is essential. These transformers are fully enclosed, preventing the ingress of external materials, and are non-ventilated, which means they do not rely on airflow for cooling. As a result, TENV transformers are highly efficient and require less maintenance, making them ideal for industrial applications, urban environments, and outdoor installations.</p><p>The TENV transformers market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period.
