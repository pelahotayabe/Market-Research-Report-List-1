<p><p>Antifog agents are categorized into short-term and long-term types based on their duration of effectiveness. Short-term antifog agents provide immediate fog-reducing effects and are typically used in situations requiring quick clarity, such as in lenses or protective gear. Long-term antifog agents, on the other hand, offer prolonged protection against fogging, making them suitable for permanent applications like eyewear coatings or automotive windshields. Choosing between these types depends on the specific usage requirements and the desired longevity of the antifog effect.</p></p>
