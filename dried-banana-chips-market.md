<p><p>The dried banana chips market is segmented into three main types: freeze-dried, vacuum-dried, and others. Freeze-dried banana chips retain their nutrients and flavor while achieving a light and crispy texture, making them popular among health-conscious consumers. Vacuum drying involves removing moisture under low pressure, resulting in a product with a longer shelf life and preserved taste. The "others" category encompasses traditional sun-dried and oven-dried methods, which vary in texture and flavor but may not offer the same nutrient retention as the first two types.</p></p>
