<p><p>The personal 3D printer market is characterized by several key technologies. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) uses thermoplastic filaments, making it popular for hobbyists due to its affordability. Poly-jet printing delivers high-resolution parts by depositing resin layers, ideal for intricate designs. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) utilizes lasers to fuse powdered materials, producing strong, durable items, often used in engineering applications. Stereolithography (SLA) employs ultraviolet light to cure resin, offering high precision. Other market types may include innovative or hybrid printing technologies, expanding capabilities and applications.</p></p>
