<p><p>The Styrene-Acrylonitrile (SAN) resin market can be categorized into three primary types based on the polymerization method: </p><p>1. **Suspension Polymerization**: This method yields high molecular weight resins with excellent clarity and impact resistance. </p><p>2. **Emulsion Polymerization**: Producing finer particles, this approach enhances processability and minimizes solvent use, offering improved thermal properties. </p><p>3. **Continuous Mass Polymerization**: This technique ensures consistent quality and higher efficiency in production, making it suitable for large-scale applications. Each type serves distinct requirements in various industries.</p></p>
