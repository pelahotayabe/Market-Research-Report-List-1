<p><p>The dust extraction system market is divided into two main types: wet and dry dust extraction systems. Wet dust extraction systems use water or liquid to capture and contain dust particles, effectively reducing airborne pollutants and minimizing fire hazards. They are ideal for managing fine dust generated in industries like metalworking. In contrast, dry dust extraction systems utilize filters and mechanical methods to trap dust without moisture, making them suitable for environments where water usage is impractical or unwanted. Each type is designed to enhance workplace safety and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
